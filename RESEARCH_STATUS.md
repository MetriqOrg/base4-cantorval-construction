## Meaning of terms

**Internal proof audit** means review conducted within the Metriq/PRISM research process. It is not independent peer review.

**Exact computational verification** means that a finite identity, certificate, enumeration, or consistency check was reproduced using deterministic computation. It does not establish the validity of unencoded mathematical reasoning or novelty.

**Independent review** requires examination by a qualified reviewer who was not responsible for producing the candidate result.

**Peer reviewed** is reserved for work accepted through a recognized scholarly peer-review process.

## Corrections and withdrawals

Material errors will be documented publicly. A superseded PDF will not be silently replaced under the same version number. Corrected releases receive a new version, an updated changelog, and a statement describing the effect of the correction.
