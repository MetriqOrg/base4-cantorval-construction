The paper gives a Bessel-factorization proof of Mathar's conjectured recurrence for the Type-ace lattice-walk sequence OEIS A302186. Exact reproducibility checks reconstruct the coefficients, verify both the conjectured and proved recurrences through `n=80`, and confirm the Ore-operator factorization. These checks do not constitute independent peer review or an exhaustive priority search.
